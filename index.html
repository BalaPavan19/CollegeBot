Admissions

  • Admission to the B.Tech program is done on the basis of rank secured by a candidate in AP EAMCET examination conducted by JNTU, Kakinada. Admission is also done through national level JEE Main examination, conducted by CBSE.
  • Admission to the M.Tech programme is done on the basis of merit in AP PGECET examination conducted by Andhra University, Visakhapatnam. Admission is also done through National level GATE Examination conducted by IISC and IITs.
